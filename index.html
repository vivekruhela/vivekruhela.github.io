About

What I’m working on

My current focus is building a robust, ancestry-aware ROH → variant → gene pipeline for Alzheimer’s disease and multi-cohort replication. I’m particularly interested in (1) parameter-stable ROH consensus, (2) rare-variant burden in ROH segments, and (3) integrating eQTL and chromatin signals for prioritization.

Beyond AD, I explore transferable architectures from oncology (e.g., MM gene-panel design) to neurodegeneration, emphasizing reproducibility, open data, and explainable AI.
